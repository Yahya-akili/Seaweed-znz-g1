From Ocean to Your Home

Discover the journey of our Zanzibar seaweed products, from sustainable harvesting to careful processing that preserves all the natural benefits.

Our Production Process

1

Sustainable Harvesting

Seaweed is carefully hand-harvested by local farmers during optimal growth periods to ensure regeneration.

2

Sun-Drying

Harvested seaweed is naturally sun-dried on raised racks to preserve nutrients without artificial heat.

3

Extraction & Processing

Active compounds are extracted using cold-press and low-temperature methods to maintain potency.

4

Product Formulation

Seaweed extracts are combined with other natural ingredients to create our soaps, oils, and jellies.
